Which movie was the most profitable?

Dataset Source: Zenodo


Total revenues for top 20 profitable movies

Revenue to cost ratios for top 20 profitable movies

Topic description: Every year, numerous Hollywood movies are released which compete with each other to be successful on box office. Their success and profitability is judged by various factors and metrics. Here, we have tried to argue the profitability of different movies on the basis of two different metrics such as total revenue and revenue to cost ratio.

Revenue chart
The revenue chart shown above on the left shows that Avatar earned the highest revenue and hence is the most profitable movie compared to the rest. The rhetoric techniques used here are:
  • Omission: The cost (budget) of making the movie is not taken into account and profitability is based solely on total revenue.
  • Metonymy: Selection of revenue metric which proves Avatar to be the most profitable movie is done.
  • Data provenance: Link to dataset is given to establish credibility.
  • Anchoring: The bar for Avatar is marked as red to depict significance visually.
Revenue to cost ratio chart
The revenue to cost ratio chart shown above on the right shows that Minions is the most profitable movie compared to the rest because it has the highest revenue/cost ratio. The rhetoric techniques used here are:
  • Metonymy: Selection of revenue/cost metric which proves Minions to be the most profitable movie is done.
  • Data provenance: Link to dataset is given to establish credibility.
  • Anchoring: The bar for Minions is marked as red to depict significance visually.
